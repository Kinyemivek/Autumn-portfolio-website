About Me

I’m a passionate nature photographer based in the vibrant city of Lagos, Nigeria. From the lush rainforests of Erin-Ijesha to the serene beaches of Tarkwa Bay, I find endless inspiration in the diverse landscapes and rich biodiversity that our beautiful country has to offer.

Photography has been my lifelong passion, allowing me to capture the intricate details and breathtaking fine dinstinctions that often go unnoticed. With a keen eye for composition and a deep appreciation for the natural world, I strive to showcase the beauty and resilience of Nigeria’s ecosystems through my lens. Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of working on various projects that highlight conservation efforts, celebrate cultural connections to nature, and bring awareness to environmental challenges.

To learn more about my work, check out my work. Thanks for stopping by!
